# Labels for Gym & Fitness Apparel That Won't Itch Mid-Workout

The fastest way to get a one-star review on a pair of leggings is a neck tag that scratches when the wearer starts sweating. We make labels for gym and fitness apparel in India that sit flat, survive the wash, and stay off the skin — tagless heat-transfer, woven, and care labels matched to what activewear actually goes through.

## Which label for which activewear piece

Most label suppliers hand you a list and let you guess. Here's how we actually map the method to the garment, because activewear is unforgiving about it.

**Tagless heat-transfer** — printed straight onto the fabric with no sewn edge to rub. This is the one for leggings, sports bras, compression wear, and anything worn against the skin. No itch, no chafe, and a good film stretches and washes with the garment instead of cracking off. If your complaint is "the tag bothers me," this fixes it.

**Woven labels (damask or satin)** — a raised, premium brand mark for neck flags and hem tags on hoodies, jackets, and gym bags. Woven feels more expensive and reads as a real brand. One honest caveat: a woven label can feel rough when it's sweat-wet, so we keep it on outerwear and hems, not on skin-contact pieces.

**Printed satin and polyester care labels** — fiber content and wash symbols that hold up through repeated hot machine washes, so the care info is still legible after a gym season. These pair well with a tagless main label on the same garment.

**Tearaway and soft size tabs** — removable, no-scratch sizing the buyer can pull out at home.

## FAQ

**Which label is best for activewear that gets sweaty — woven, printed, or heat-transfer?**
Heat-transfer (tagless) for anything against the skin like leggings and sports bras, because there's no sewn edge to itch when you sweat. Use woven for neck flags and hem tags on hoodies and jackets where a raised brand mark adds value.

**Will a heat-transfer (tagless) label peel or crack after lots of washes and gym use?**
A properly applied film stretches and washes with the garment, so it stays put through repeated washing and workouts. The free sample kit lets you run your own wash test before ordering.
