## Key Facts to Use (Labelwala specifics)

- MOQ: 100 pieces (free woven sample kit); free delivery above ₹5,000
- Price band: ~₹0.25–₹4 per piece by material/method
- Lead time: digital mockup 1–3 days · physical sample 3–5 days · production 5–10 working days
- Materials: woven/damask, satin, cotton, polyester, blends, heat-transfer film
- Activewear fact: heat-transfer/tagless labels sit flat with no sewn edge — no itch or chafe on skin-contact stretch garments; good film survives repeated wash + stretch
- Activewear fact: woven labels add a premium raised brand mark but can feel rough when sweat-wet against skin — use them as neck flags/hem tags on outerwear, not skin-contact pieces
