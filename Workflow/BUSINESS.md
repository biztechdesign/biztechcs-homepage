## What This Means for Content

- **Show, don't tell.** Lead with **outcomes and named proof**, not "Global Leader" claims.
  Trust = named clients + quantified outcomes + certifications + contracts — NOT years/headcount alone.
- **Positioning line:** *"Enterprise software, delivered with US/EU accountability and global engineering scale."*
- Technical vocabulary is **enterprise software**: AI agents/LLM/RAG, ERP/Odoo/Dynamics/Salesforce,
  headless commerce/Magento/Shopify, AWS/Azure/CI-CD/SRE, ISO 27001/9001, WCAG, Core Web Vitals.
- **De-risk language** for the offshore concern: ISO certs, IP transfer, US/EU presence + time-zone
  overlap, senior-engineer ratio, no cost overruns, transparent Agile delivery.
- **Never** use: "world-class", "one-stop solution", "cutting-edge", "seamless", "unlock", "elevate
  your brand", "Global Leader", "industry-leading", rhetorical-question heroes, or any banned AI words
  (delve, leverage, utilize, robust, holistic, comprehensive, game-changer).
- Currency/region framing is **USD and Western buyers** (not ₹/India hobbyists).
